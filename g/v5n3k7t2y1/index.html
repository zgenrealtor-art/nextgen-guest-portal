Must See

Baths & Spas
Széchenyi (Városliget, Metro M1), Rudas (Döbrentei tér), Gellért (Gellért tér).
Top Pick
Heroes' Square / Hősök Tere
One of the major squares, famous for the Millennium Monument. Museums and Városliget nearby.
The Castle
Walk up from Lánchíd/Chain Bridge or take the funicular. Fisherman's Bastion, Matthias church, Labyrinth.
Top Pick
Parliament
Beautiful from outside. Guided tours available inside.
Great Market Hall
End of Váci utca, close to Szabadság bridge. Best quality Hungarian food — salamis, goose liver.
The Great Synagogue
At Dohány utca 2 — the biggest synagogue in Europe. St. Stephen's Basilica at Szent István square — great view from the top.
Opera House
Andrássy út 22 — one of Budapest's most beautiful avenues, full of embassies.
Danube & Bridges
The most beautiful part is between Erzsébet and Lánchíd — the oldest bridge in Budapest.
Vörösmarty tér
Where Váci street ends. In December — famous Christmas fair.
Giant Wheel
At Erzsébet tér — a bit like the London Eye (might not operate all year).

Hidden Gems

Pinball Museum
13th district, Radnóti Miklós utca 18. Over 130 pinball machines to play!
Hospital in the Rock
4/C Lovas út, 1st district. WWII hospital in caves under Castle Hill. Now a fascinating museum.
Gül Baba's Tomb
14 Mecset utca, 2nd district. Turkish monk's tomb with a nice park around.
Escape Rooms
Very popular in Hungary. Great fun, especially for groups!
Károlyi Kert
Nice small park in the middle of downtown, next to Magyar utca.
Museum of Illusions
Great fun for the whole family.
Uránia Film Theatre
21 Rákóczi út — the most beautiful cinema in Budapest. 19th century café upstairs.
Botanical Garden / Füvészkert
25 Illés utca, district 8. Hundred-year-old trees, beautiful flowers. Bring a blanket for a picnic!
Hidden Mini Statues
10-20 cm tall statues hidden around Budapest by Mihály Kolodko. A great treasure hunt! Search for 'Kolodko statues'.

For Kids & Families

Budapest Zoo
In the City Park (Városliget). Seal shows, gorillas, elephants.
Tropicarium
Shark zoo in Campona mall, 22nd district. Mainly water life.
Kockapark / Brickpark
Lego exhibition with Lego installations, also in Campona mall.
